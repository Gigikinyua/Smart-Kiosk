About Kiosk Smart

Rekodi | Nunua | Kuza biashara

Ksmart provides kiosks & vibandasky owners with an easy to use purchase, record keeping and credit platform. Users of the product place all purchase orders from their local favorite wholesalers thus providing a record trail for use in providing credit from the wholesalers.

Users can also use the records to obtain credit from other financial institutions such as banks & sacco and other financial institutions.
